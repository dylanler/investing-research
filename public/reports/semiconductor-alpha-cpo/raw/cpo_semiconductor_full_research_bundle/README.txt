CPO Semiconductor Alpha Research Bundle

Contents:
- cpo_semiconductor_alpha_map.html: interactive HTML research page with ranking, network/mind-map visuals, charts, source trail, cash-flow caveats, and relationship explorer.
- cpo_semiconductor_alpha_master_flat.csv: master flat file with company, relationship, cash-flow, alpha-reasoning, and source columns.
- cpo_semiconductor_alpha_companies.csv: 100-company alpha ranking, 50 US public semiconductor stocks and 50 non-US public semiconductor stocks.
- cpo_semiconductor_relationship_edges.csv: relationship graph edges, with at least 20 direct connections per company.
- cpo_semiconductor_source_index.csv: source index used in the research page/CSV bundle.
- cpo_semiconductor_cash_flow_audit.csv: disclosed and inferred cash-flow/revenue-stream audit.
- cpo_cable_external_watchlist.csv: public/private CPO-cable/interconnect watchlist outside the strict semiconductor-company universe.
- source_tweet_screenshot.png: screenshot that initiated the research prompt.

Notes:
- This is research material, not investment advice.
- Pairwise semiconductor revenue streams are often not disclosed by companies; rows are labeled by disclosure strength where relevant.
